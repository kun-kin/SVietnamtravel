This tour is designed for travellers desiring to discover World Heritage Sites in Vietnam, Cambodia and Laos. 14 days of the trip is chance for you to visit World Heritage sites in Vietnam including Ha Long bay, Hue ancient capital and Hoi An ancient town. You also visit The Wonder Word Angkor complex in Siem Reap (Cambodia) as well as Luang Prabang town - the heart of Laostiane culture.
